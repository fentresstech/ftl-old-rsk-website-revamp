Repair

Providing the highest quality appliance repair service to the greater Portland area.

On-Site Repair

Most appliance repairs can be handled at your location. We strive to complete repairs in fewer than two hours so you can get back to work. If the repair requires additional attention, we will transport the appliance back to our repair center.

Drop Off

Our full service repair center is open eight hours a day, allowing you to walk-in at your convenience. Our staff will take the time to discuss all repair options with you, and ensure your appliances are back in working order as soon as possible.

24/7 Support

Unexpected appliance failures can happen anytime, which is why our on-call staff is available 24/7. In addition to 24/7 support by calling (503) 241-8318, we have a Contact Us from that is monitored daily.
